About Janma Jayanthi

Swami's birth star falls between mid-November and mid-December each year, on Virchiga Ravi and Aarudra Nakshatra. SSSVSS marks this auspicious day with special prayers, Abhishekam, bhajans, and collective devotion. Country-level celebrations for Swami's Birthday are also participated in by SSSVSS members.
