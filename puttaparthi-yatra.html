About the Yatra

Puttaparthi Yatra

SSSVSS organises pilgrimage trips to Prasanthi Nilayam — the holy ashram of Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba in Puttaparthi, Andhra Pradesh, India. The Yatra typically coincides with major events at Prasanthi Nilayam. Trip details, dates, and registration are announced via the WhatsApp group.

Want to host or need details? Contact the initiative lead directly or reach our WhatsApp admins.
