About

90 Days of Sadhana

In the 90 days preceding Sri Gayatri Maha Yagna and Swami's Janma Jayanthi, SSSVSS conducts a daily digital morning prayer session. Devotees join online via a digital altar hosted by a volunteer, beginning each day with collective chanting and prayer.

Daily Structure

Morning Programme

Each 30-minute session follows the same daily programme:

6:48 AM
Sai Ashtottram
6:53 AM
Gayatri Mantra
6:57 AM
Mangal Arathi
6:59 AM
Sai Gayatri
Prior to SGMY

90-Day Gayatri Japa Sadhana

Alongside the daily morning prayers, devotees are invited to take up a Gayatri Japa Sadhana — chanting the Gayatri Mantra daily and submitting counts for the collective SGMY offering. See the GMY 2026 page for the Japa count form and running totals.
