About Akhanda Bhajans

Akhanda Bhajans is a country-level annual event in which SSSVSS participates alongside other Sai organisations across Singapore. The 24-hour unbroken bhajan session begins at 6:00 PM on Saturday and concludes at 6:00 PM on Sunday on the second weekend of November. Devotees sign up for singing slots to ensure the bhajans never stop.
