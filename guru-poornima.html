About Guru Poornima

Guru Poornima falls on the full moon day of the Hindu month of Ashadha. It is the most sacred day to honour the Guru — the one who removes the darkness of ignorance. SSSVSS celebrates this day with Guru Vandana, special Veda Parayanam, and bhajans in gratitude to Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba, our eternal Guru.
