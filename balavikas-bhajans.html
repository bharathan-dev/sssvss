All Balavikas and Satyanadam students are encouraged to participate. Families and other devotees are welcome to attend and support the children.

Children rehearse bhajans during their weekly Balavikas classes. The initiative lead coordinates the programme with the Balavikas teachers.

Primarily children aged 5–12 in the Balavikas programme. Teenvikas students may also participate.
