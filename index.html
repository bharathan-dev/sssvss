"The vibrations produced by the utterance of the Mantra, uniting with the cosmic nāda in the universe, become one with Universal Consciousness." — Sri Sathya Sai Speaks, Vol. 17 · October 1984

Sri Sathya Sai Vedic Society Singapore (SSSVSS) was formed as an informal group conducting monthly Ekadasa Rudram — Sri Rudram chanted 11 times by 11 persons on Nakshatra Arudhra, the birth star of Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba.

Today the Society runs 15 regular activities, 8 annual events, 10 weekly digital Vedic classes, and community service — all rooted in Swami's five human values.

Sathya · Truth
Speak truth with love
Dharma · Righteousness
Act with integrity
Shanti · Peace
Cultivate inner stillness
Prema · Love
Serve all with devotion
Ahimsa · Non-Violence
Harm no living being in thought, word, or deed
