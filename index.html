"The vibrations produced by the utterance of the Mantra, uniting with the cosmic nāda in the universe, become one with Universal Consciousness." — Sri Sathya Sai Speaks, Vol. 17 · October 1984

Sri Sathya Sai Vedic Society Singapore (SSSVSS) was formed as an informal group conducting monthly Ekadasa Rudram — the chanting of Sri Rudram 11 times by 11 persons — to celebrate Nakshatra Arudhra, the birth star of Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba.

Today the Society runs regular devotional activities, annual yagnas, Vedic classes for all ages, and community service — all rooted in Swami's five human values.

Sathya · Truth

Dharma · Righteousness
Act with integrity
Shanti · Peace

The Veda School of SSSVSS offers classes from Tuesday to Sunday, covering Vedic programmes for children, youth, and adults. Our dedicated Gurus guide each student with patience, devotion, and deep knowledge of the tradition.

All classes are held in Singapore. New batches start periodically throughout the year — all levels welcome, from complete beginners to advanced practitioners.
